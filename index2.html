About us

WE ARE CREATIVE

Makandi is a Meru name with different meanings, among them a go-getter and daring person. To a Makandi nothing is impossible, she will go where no one dares to go. With that knowledge would you be surprised why I would go from one thriving career to another and leave at the peek of them all? With over a decade experience in corporate United Kingdom ranging from Retail, Ministry Of Defence (UK), Telecommunications, Management and Hospitality – I have gathered major skills, experiences and with my charisma you may dare call me a jack of all trades. I am a go-getter and not afraid to start all over because in that starting, I know better and will be better.

With time and occupation in different fields, I realised that I turned out to be great at teaching and always wanted to help others release there full potential and a higher self – a better version of themselves. Seeing people in full potential brought soo much satisfaction. That is when the penny dropped! It is my passion and talent brought to one. I always had it just didn’t know what it was. Life coaching is about creating the right mindset and to help you fill the gaps in our master plan and bring about clarity and accountability. Life Coaching is action and future focused.

My work

How I Work

I hold most of my sessions online and ocassional events which will be communicated on the website and and social media pages.

My approach is use of a combination of mindset and practical tools with intuition to bring you results that will last.

As a Life Coach, I come to the partnership as a facilitator to challenge, inspire, empathise and to hold you accountable. I do not tell you what to do, rather I support, encourage and motivate you reach your desired goal.

I have been there like you, tried things that didn’t work, wasted money and resources only to get frustrated. I stand for 3 main things: CLARITY, INTEGRITY and STABILITY

What Is included in Our Programs

Coaching sessions

Depending on your package, you will have online coaching session each month focused on your goals and topics geared towards your goals an growth.

Assessment

We begin with a psychometric/personality test to help us bring full awareness and focus more on your goal and a plan.

Personalised plan

After your assessment we create a coaching plan so you can monitor your progress and stay on track.

Unlimited email support

You have unlimited email support in between session if you need support or share a win.

Homework and Tools

Tools and exercises will guide you through the desired change and give you fresh insights and help you take action.

Session notes

A summary of your coaching action will be sent to you at the end of each one to one sesion to bring you accountability and keep you in track.
